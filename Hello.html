About BMW M3 Competition

The BMW M3 Competition is a high-performance sedan that blends luxury with thrilling driving dynamics.

Powered by a 3.0-liter twin-turbo inline-six engine, it delivers 503 horsepower and 479 lb-ft of torque, offering blistering acceleration and precision handling. Its rear-wheel-drive setup, with an available xDrive all-wheel-drive option, ensures optimal traction and control.

The M3 Competition features an aggressive design with aerodynamic enhancements, a bold kidney grille, and premium interior finishes. Advanced technology, including adaptive suspension and customizable drive modes, makes it a versatile machine.
